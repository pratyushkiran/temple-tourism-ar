Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple, built in the 13th century in Odisha, India, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its stunning architecture resembling a colossal chariot dedicated to the Sun God, Surya. Its intricate stone carvings and massive wheels showcase the pinnacle of Kalinga artistry.

Raja Rani Temple

The Raja Rani Temple, built in the 11th century in Odisha, India, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings. Unlike other temples in the region, it is dedicated to both Shiva and Shakti, showcasing a unique blend of artistic styles. The temple's serene ambiance and exquisite sculptures make it a must-visit for art and history enthusiasts.
